Membership categories

Annual Fees Payable
There shall be payable to the organization an annual subscription (differentiated in respect of the categories identified below) as shall be determined from time to time by the BoD and ratified by the General Assembly meeting of the organization. The fees payable for the various categories of membership are as follows:

Annual payable registration fees for members within Africa
STUDENT MEMBER
$ 25

Any person who is officially enrolled in a tertiary education institution. Proof of enrollment is required.

STANDARD MEMBER
$ 50

Any individual person involved in the higher education sector and who has interests in the goals and objectives of ANIE.

AFFILIATE MEMBER
$ 100

Any organization that has similar goals and objectives as ANIE and offers reciprocal membership and commitment to information sharing. These are partners from whom ANIE can draw benefits by association.

Annual payable registration fees for International members
STUDENT MEMBER
$ 50

Any person who is officially enrolled in a tertiary education institution. Proof of enrollment is required.

STANDARD MEMBER
$ 100

Any individual person involved in the higher education sector and who has interests in the goals and objectives of ANIE.

AFFILIATE MEMBER
$ 200

Any organization that has similar goals and objectives as ANIE and offers reciprocal membership and commitment to information sharing. These are partners from whom ANIE can draw benefits by association.
